The Gryffindor Paradigm

Research as Advocacy

Driven by a moral imperative, this paradigm uses research as a tool for social justice. It champions the cause of the marginalized and seeks to create tangible, positive change. The core question is not just 'what is wrong?' but 'how can we fix it?'.

The Hufflepuff Paradigm

Research as Collaboration

This paradigm prioritizes fairness, inclusivity, and process. It views research as a human enterprise built on trust and mutual respect, where every voice is valued. The core belief is that sound outcomes emerge from a sound, collaborative foundation.

The Integrated Inquirer: The Hatstall Dilemma

The most complex challenges require an integrated approach, blending the strengths of all four houses. A "Hatstall" researcher moves fluidly between paradigms. Here's how they might tackle a multifaceted problem like climate change.

Complex Problem: Climate Change Mitigation

Gryffindor's Role

Conducts advocacy research with impacted communities to drive policy change.

Hufflepuff's Role

Uses collaborative methods to co-design local adaptation strategies with stakeholders.

Ravenclaw's Role

Applies rigorous analysis to climate data to model patterns and build theories.

Slytherin's Role

Identifies high-leverage intervention points to influence corporate and government actors.
